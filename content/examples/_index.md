title: Examples
---

A few examples of what CamillaDSP can be used for.

- [Active crossovers](active-crossovers) — split a signal into frequency bands and route each to
  a separate amplifier and driver
- [Room correction](room-correction) — apply FIR correction filters to compensate for acoustic
  problems
- [Advanced DSP](advanced-dsp) — mix channels, add resampling, apply dynamic loudness, and chain
  filter stages
